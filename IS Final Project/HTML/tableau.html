As we make our way into Tableau, the true question remains: How are profits faring in the 50 states?

And according to our live Tableau it would appear that California is leading the nation in profits with $76,381. Let us, however, take a deep dive into California and see just how they have performed over the course of the last two years.

Here is a Tableau to help us find that:

Over the course of the past two years recorded in this data, it can be seen that profits have traditionally been lower at the beginning of each calendar year but pick up towards the end of the year.

Now let's break it down by product to see which product is the most profitable in the state of California.

From the graph presented it is clear that the most profitable product in California is Accessories.

How unexpected.

Who would have thought that people in California like to accessorize.

But now let's go ahead and see what sales for accessories would look like if we projected sales through November of 2015.

The Tableau forecast for profits made off of accessories is fairly moderate, the lighter blue area representing a 95% confidence rate.
